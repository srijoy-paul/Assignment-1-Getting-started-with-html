1. Tags in HTML:
Basically, tags in html are used to structure our webpage and to define how our content should be displayed. The tag names are enclosed within '<' and '>' bracket. There are opening and closing of a tag.
There are two types of tags:
i.) <tag_name></tag_name>
ex: <p></p>

ii.) <tag_name/>
ex: <br/>

2. Elements in HTML:
Basically, the opening tag, the content, the closing tag, together as a whole is considered as elements in html.
ex:
<h1>Hello!</h1> 
The whole thing written in the previous line, is called an element.
here, <h1> is the opening tag.
     Hello! is the content.
     </h1> is the closing tag.
